Types of Baseball

Beginner Baseball

Focuses on learning the rules, basic throwing, catching, and batting. Games are shorter and often played with simplified scoring to help newcomers enjoy the sport without pressure.

Intermediate Baseball

Players start refining techniques like pitching styles, batting strategies, and defensive plays. Team coordination becomes more important, and games follow standard rules.

Advanced Baseball

Competitive level with full rules, advanced tactics, and specialized positions. Players focus on speed, accuracy, and strategy to perform at a higher level.
